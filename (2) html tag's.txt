15. SEO Tag's:-

canonical tag:-
ek hi content ko do page maan rakha ho to google rank nahin karta is liay cononical tag say google ko btatay hain
dono page maan same content ha cononcial waly ko zada targi doo.

robots tag :-
index matlab search page maan dikhna ha za nahin no index likhan gay to nahin dikhay ga.
follow matlab jo link lagay hain un ko search maan follow karo external link lagay hon to nofollow

<title>home page</title>
    <meta name="description" content="this is a page about new car"/>
    <meta name="keywords" content="new , car"/>
    <link rel="canonical" href="car.html" />
    <meta name="robots" content="index,follow" />
